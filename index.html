- What We Do -

At Sexual Health Dublin, we pair you with a medical service or professional based on your responses to a quick questionnaire, including but not limited to services regarding STIs or STDs, pregnancy or family planning, PrEP! After you fill out our questionnaire, we will be in contact within 48 to 72 business hours, at which point you can choose to set up an appointment with the service we have suggested, or with one of our friendly staff for further assistance. We also have professionals on call from 9:00 until 18:00 GST, Monday through Friday (excluding Bank Holidays).

*Please note: Due to the current COVID-19 crisis, some services may not be functioning as advertised here. We are doing our best to keep our information up to date, and we appreciate your continued patience as we all fight this battle.*

*If you become aware of a listed medical professional who has either changed or suspended their services until such a time as Covid has come under control, please reach out to us and we will update our information as soon as we've verified the new information - we're all in this together!*

- Get Started -

To get started with Sexual Health Dublin, please take a few moments to fill out our questionnaire! Once you fill it out and submit it, someone from our team of professionals will get to work pairing you with three options for healthcare. The more detail you provide for us, the more we can do for you! You will recieve an email from us in the next 2 to 3 business days with our suggestions based on your answers. If you are for some reason unsatisfied with our suggestions, please feel free to contact us and one of our professionals will work with you to find another service that suits you better.
